A Message from our Director

Envision Dance Collective offers intermediate-advanced dancers the opportunity to cultivate their art through pre-professional training, audition experiences, real-world connections, and performance opportunities. We aspire to be a gathering that challenges dancers of all ages to put their best work on the floor, and our goal is to train the next generation of leaders in the art of dance. We hope to see you at our next national convention. Claire Grant Owner and Artistic Director
